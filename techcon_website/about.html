History

The history of technology is a vast and fascinating journey, stretching back millennia. Our ancestors' first foray into technology involved crafting tools from stone, wood, and bone. These basic implements revolutionized hunting, gathering, and food preparation. Mastering fire was a game-changer. It provided warmth, protection from predators, a means to cook food, and a tool for crafting implements.

Mission

To improve the human condition: This is a broad goal that encompasses many specific objectives, such as making our lives easier, healthier, and more fulfilling. Technology can help us solve problems related to food production, disease, communication, transportation, and more.
